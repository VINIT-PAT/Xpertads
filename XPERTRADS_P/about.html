FAQs

  • As a scan centre owner I want to know what is the workflow of telereporting of your company?

    We have tied up with teleradiology software companies who will install a “receiver” on your desktop connected by LAN to your workstation through which we will receive the software from our end automatically(or manually depending on settings you want). Our assistant will cordinate with doctors to get the scan reported within the stipulated time. Once reported it will flash on your screen, as reported and you can directly take printout. We can provide demo procedure as per your convenience.

  • I need to get good quality drafted reports only. Is that possible?

    Yes we do provide good quality drafted reports. You can either send the scan through gmail or via software as described in FAQ 1. We will assist you for the same.

  • I have done my scan at a centre but want your expert second opinion. Whats the procedure?

    A CD of the scan must be procured from the centre where scan was performed.The scan can then be mailed to us in zip format or uploaded by pressing on tab provided on the website “Upload the scan”. You will get report via mail in 24 hours. In case of any difficulty in sending scan to us we will guide u for the same.
